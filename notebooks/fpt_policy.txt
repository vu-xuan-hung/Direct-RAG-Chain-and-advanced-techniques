FPT INTERNAL POLICY (EXTENDED VERSION)

---

Document ID: FPT-HR-001
Effective Date: 01/01/2024
Scope: Applies to all full-time employees at FPT and its affiliated units.

---

1. WORKING HOURS

* Standard working time: 8 hours/day, Monday to Friday.
* Morning: 08:30 - 12:00
* Lunch break: 12:00 - 13:30
* Afternoon: 13:30 - 17:30

Additional Rules:

* Employees must be present at least 5 minutes before working hours.
* Flexible working hours may be applied depending on department approval.
* Remote work must be approved by direct manager.

---

2. ATTENDANCE REGULATION

* Employees must check-in/check-out via myFPT system:

  * Facial recognition
  * Employee card
* Attendance data is the official basis for payroll and discipline.

Late/Early Leave:

* Late after 08:30 or leaving before 17:30 without approval is considered a violation.

Penalty:

* < 15 minutes: Warning
* 15 - < 30 minutes: 50,000 VND fine
* ≥ 30 minutes: Deduct 0.5 leave day or salary

Additional Rules:

* More than 5 late times/month → KPI impact
* Attendance fraud → strict disciplinary action

---

3. OVERTIME

* OVERTIME must:

  * Be required by work
  * Be approved in advance

OVERTIME Rates:

* Weekdays: 150%
* Weekends: 200%
* Holidays: 300%

Additional Rules:

* Must comply with labor law limits
* Unauthorized OVERTIME → not paid

Document ID: FPT-HR-002
Effective Date: 01/01/2024

---

1. ANNUAL LEAVE

* 12 days/year for 12-month contract
* +1 day every 5 years of service

Usage:

* Must be registered and approved

Carry-over:

* Max 5 days to Q1 next year
* Expired leave will be removed
* Not convertible to cash

---

2. SICK LEAVE

* Max 30 days/year (covered by social insurance)

Requirements:

* Medical certificate required within 3 days after return
* More than 2 consecutive days → mandatory proof

---

3. MATERNITY / PATERNITY LEAVE

Female Employees:

* 6 months leave
* 100% salary (social insurance)
* +20% support (first 3 months)

Male Employees:

* 5 days (normal birth)
* 7–14 days (C-section or multiple births)

---

4. SPECIAL LEAVE

* Marriage: 3 days
* Funeral: 3–5 days
* Unpaid leave: Requires approval

---

Document ID: FPT-SEC-003
Effective Date: 01/01/2024

---

1. COMPANY ASSETS PROTECTION

* Devices are for work purposes only
* Strictly prohibited:

  * Cracked software
  * Unknown sources

Security Rules:

* Password ≥ 12 characters
* Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters
* Change every 90 days
* No account sharing

---

2. INTERNAL DATA HANDLING

Prohibited:

* Sending internal data via personal email
* Uploading to personal cloud storage

Sensitive Data:

* Source code
* Customer information
* Internal documents

Violation:

* Immediate termination
* Compensation liability
* Legal action if necessary

---

3. REMOTE WORK / DEVICE USAGE

* Must use FPT VPN
* Only approved devices allowed

Additional Rules:

* Avoid public unsecured WiFi
* Device must have:

  * Antivirus
  * Firewall
  * Disk encryption

---

4. SECURITY INCIDENT MANAGEMENT

* Any incident must be reported immediately:

  * Data breach
  * Malware / virus

---

GENERAL TERMS

* All employees must comply with this policy.
* Violations will be handled according to:

  * Company regulations
  * Vietnamese labor law
* Policy may be updated without prior notice.
